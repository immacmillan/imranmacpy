Let me share a little about myself, from one human to another

Growing up in a suburb outside of the DC Metropolitan area, I always had a penchant for sports, technology and cars.

That quickly budded into a passion for engineering and technology, and ultimately led me to pursue a degree in Chemical & Biomolecular Engineering from the University of Pennsylvania.

Professionally, I quickly segued into business and technology consulting after graduation. That led me through stints working internationally throughout Africa, Europe and the Middle-East.

Ultimately, I found my way back to the DC area - and continued to learn technologies in the data analytics, software development, and IT architecture domains.

Currently, I Consult for Deloitte delivering Enterprise IT Architecture Services, Software Dev, Data Analytics and Product Management.


On a less professional note, I still take the time to pursue my passions at every passing opportunity!

"I truly believe that time spent nourishing oneself through connection to nature and physical exertion is second to none"

"It is a pity to live life not knowing the capabilities of one's own body and mind"

Of the outdoor activities I enjoy, snowboarding has got to be one of my favorites. The feeling of first lines in fresh powder is akin to flying weightlessly throw a river of snow.

Following closely behind are my three favorite competitive sports - tennis, basketball and football.

Things I've tried and would love to improve on include kite surfing, and surfing in general now that I'm based in California.

Beyond adrenaline seeking adventure sports - I'm always keen on a good hike, exploring a scenic vista, or meandering down a coastal byway.

You can find me most days either at my latpop, or the gym - in between a mtn-bike ride in the Ord and my daily commute to the Presidio.
