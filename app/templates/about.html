Let me share a little about myself, from one human to another

Growing up in a suburb outside of the DC Metropolitan area, I always had a penchant for sports, technology and cars.

That quickly budded into a passion for engineering and technology, and ultimately led me to pursue a degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of Pennsylvania

Professionally, I quickly segued into business and technology consulting after graduation. That led me through stints working internationally throughout Africa, Europe and the Middle-East.

Ultimately, I found my way back to the DC area - and continued to learn technologies in the data analytics, software development, and IT architecture domains

Now I work in IT Services for Deloitte, in the capacity of a Senior Consultant.


On a less professional note, I still take the time to pursue my outdoor passions at every passing opportunity

I truly believe that time spent nourishing yourself through connection to nature and physical exertion is second to none.

It is truly a pity to live life not knowing the capabilities of one's own body and mind.

Of the outdoor activities I enjoy, snowboarding has got to be one of my favorites.

But following closely behind are my three favorite sports for tennis, basketball and football.

I'm always keen on a good hike, exploring a scenic vista, or meandering down the California coast
